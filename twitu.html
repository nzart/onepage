About

I was asked to do a design for a show on our student-run television station, TigerVision. The show was a late-night talk show and that is where a lot of the inspiration for the design came from.

Because it is a student run station themes from the campus came in to play as well. I was initially asked to include library bridge into the design but I created a design featuring a silhouette of Old Main which was featured in the final, chosen design.

While the show did not last long, I enjoyed coming up with a design as well as working with the host.
